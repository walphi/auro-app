# Email — After Voice/WhatsApp Warm Acknowledgement

> **Use when:** a decision-maker replied "send details" / "tell me more" via voice or WhatsApp. Email is a fallback, not a first-touch. **Cal.com is still the CTA, never a sales deck.**

---

## ✉️ The email (≤ 6 lines)

**Subject:** `15-min look at AURO for {company}` — keep it short, no emoji

```
Hi {first_name},

Thanks for the reply — appreciate it.

Context: AURO is an AI lead-nurturing agent built for
global real estate ops teams. We work with brokerages
running 200+ agents and developers with portfolios of
similar shape.

One 15-min call is the simplest next step, here's the link:
https://cal.com/auro-app/30min

Happy to walk you through the 1-min demo live on the call.

— Phillip
pw@auroapp.com  ·  +971 (0) [your AURO direct]
```

No attachment. No brochure. No PDF deck. **Cal.com is the CTA.**

---

## ✅ Send from

`pw@auroapp.com` (the existing AURO mailbox — confirmed live on auroapp.com footer)

## 🎯 Outcomes

| What happened | New state | Next action |
|---|---|---|
| Replied confirming time | `MEETING_BOOKED` | Send WhatsApp alert to Phillip (`+971 ··· 0121`) |
| Asked for pricing | `INTERESTED_TECH` | Phillip replies personally within 4 hrs |
| Opened but no reply (7 days) | `EMAIL_OPENED_COLD` | Re-queue 30-day follow-up, don't spam |
| Bounced | `BOUNCED` | Replace with LinkedIn DM |

---

## 🚫 Never do

- ❌ Attach the AURO deck PDF — brochures are where leads go to die
- ❌ CC anyone other than the decision-maker
- ❌ Use Mailchimp / bulk senders — this email is personal to them
- ❌ "Just circling back" — never
- ❌ Reply with "did you get my last email?" — Phillip calls instead

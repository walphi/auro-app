# LinkedIn DM — Non-Callable Decision-Maker

> **Use when:** decision-maker has no direct line publicly listed, OR after WhatsApp-no-reply (state `WA_NO_REPLY`). LinkedIn is for the un-findable-by-phone, not the warm-prospect.

---

## 💬 The message (≤ 300 characters — keep it short)

```
Hi {first_name} — Phillip here, founder of AURO
(auroapp.com). We built an AI lead-nurturing agent
for global real estate ops; would love 15 minutes
to show you one workflow.

Worth a look? → https://cal.com/auro-app/30min
```

---

## ✅ Connection requests — the rule

**Never send a connection note that's longer than your DM.** Senior people reject connection requests because of the request note, not because of who sent it.

If they accept → send the DM above within 5 min, in the same message thread.

If they don't accept in 7 days → move on, don't re-request.

## 🚫 Don't

- ❌ Ask for the meeting in the **connection request** (note ≤ 200 chars, no link)
- ❌ Comment / like their posts before DMing — looks performative
- ❌ Use hashtag-heavy copy — it's LinkedIn, not Instagram
- ❌ "I'd love to pick your brain" — that is the worst opener in B2B DM

## 🎯 Outcomes

| What happened | New state | Next action |
|---|---|---|
| Replied with "yes / when" | `INTERESTED` | Send Cal.com + log |
| Sent Cal.com, no reply in 72 hrs | `LI_NO_REPLY` | Email follow-up (see email template) |
| Profile locked / no DM accepted | `REJECTED` | Cold pool — try again in 90 days |

---

## 🔗 Personalisation hook (1 fact, 1 line max)

Add **at the end** if you have one credible fact:

> *"Congratulations on the Forbes Global Properties membership"*
> *"Saw the 750-agent expansion — that's a serious ops lift"*
> *"Read your interview in Gulf News last week"*

One hook. Don't fake it.

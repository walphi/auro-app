Binghatti Skyrise is a luxury residential development by Binghatti Developers in Business Bay, Dubai.
The project features 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom apartments with premium finishes.
It offers stunning views of the Dubai Canal and the Burj Khalifa.
Amenities include a temperature-controlled swimming pool, modern gymnasium, and landscaped gardens.
The architectural design follows Binghatti's signature style with bold geometry and iconic balconies.
Completion is scheduled for Q4 2026.
Payment Plan: 70/30 (70% during construction, 30% on handover).
Prices starting from AED 1.8 Million.

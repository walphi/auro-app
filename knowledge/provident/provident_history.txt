## Company History & Founding Story
Founded in 2008 in Dubai, Provident Real Estate is one of the oldest and most trusted agencies in the UAE. Under the leadership of Founder and CEO Loai Al Fakir, we have built a nearly two-decade legacy of excellence and reliability in the Dubai property market.

## Mission & Core Values
Our mission is to provide transparent and professional real estate services. We operate with integrity and a deep commitment to trust, ensuring every client transaction is handled with the highest level of professionalism and market transparency.

## Awards & Recognition
Provident is a multi-award-winning agency, consistently recognized as a top performer. We have won "Agency of the Year" from multiple industry-leading platforms and major real estate portals in the UAE.

## Team Size & Specialization
We boast a powerhouse team of over 100 specialized real estate agents covering all major areas of Dubai. Our staff provides localized expertise and data-driven insights to ensure clients make informed investment decisions.

## Office Locations & Presence
Our headquarters are strategically located in Marina Plaza, Dubai Marina. This central location allows us to serve as a hub for premium real estate services across the Dubai Marina, Jumeirah, and the wider city.
